Who are we?

Daydream Café was founded in 2021, in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. Our café is the perfect place not only for coffee lovers, but for tea lovers and smoothie lovers as well! Whether you're on your way to work or you're looking for a place to relax while enjoying a drink, our café is the perfect place for you!
